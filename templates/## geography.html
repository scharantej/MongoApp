Physical Geography

  • Area: 1,564,116 sq km (603,946 sq mi)
  • Bordering Countries: Russia (north), China (south)
  • Highest Point: Khüiten Peak (4,374 m or 14,350 ft)
  • Lowest Point: Lake Khövsgöl (518 m or 1,700 ft)
  • Major Rivers: Selenge River, Tuul River, Orkhon River
  • Major Lakes: Lake Khövsgöl, Lake Uvs, Lake Har Us
  • Climate: Continental climate with long, cold winters and short, warm summers

Political Geography

  • Capital: Ulaanbaatar
  • Largest City: Ulaanbaatar
  • Administrative Divisions: 21 aimags (provinces) and 1 capital city
